Review exercise
===============

Review the *AWS well-architected framework* document available at:\
<https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/welcome.html>\
Identify the key pillars and their constituent best practices in
whatever format works best for you (bullet points, a map diagram
(perhaps drawn by GraphViz), a JSON document).

Look at 1 of:

-   The Azure well-architected framework:\
    <https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/architecture/framework/>

-   The Google Cloud:\
    <https://cloud.google.com/architecture/framework>

Can you map the pillars and their components onto the AWS in your
diagram / document?

Analysis
========

A discussion forum has been created on Moodle for this topic. Although
ungraded, this will help inform your project planning and documentation.

Identify what **your own 3** key principles of well-architected software
or networking/computing systems are (depending on your specialism) from
**your own** experience.

For each principle:

-   Give 2-3 examples of how **you** do this in your work.

-   Identify what difficulties can prevent this.

-   If relevant, identify specific software tools that you (intend to)
    use to assist with this principle.
